Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2025  ·  XEKIE LLC

Overview

XEKIE LLC ("XEKIE," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website xekie.com. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, password)
  • Post a XEKIE (title, description, budget, location, category)
  • Respond to a XEKIE as a seller (message and price)
  • Contact us by email

We also automatically collect certain information when you use our platform, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the XEKIE platform
  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your XEKIEs to potential sellers
  • Send you transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve our platform
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Supabase: Our backend database and authentication provider. Your account data and XEKIE posts are stored on Supabase's servers.
  • Netlify: Our hosting provider that serves the XEKIE website.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google may collect anonymized usage data.
  • Law enforcement: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of XEKIE, our users, or the public.

XEKIE posts (title, description, budget, location, category) are publicly visible to all visitors of the platform. Do not include sensitive personal information in your XEKIE posts.

Data Retention

We retain your account information and XEKIEs for as long as your account is active. You may delete your XEKIEs at any time from your dashboard. To delete your account entirely, contact us at zach@xekie.com.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your login session and analyze site usage via Google Analytics. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of XEKIE may not function properly without them.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at zach@xekie.com.

Children's Privacy

XEKIE is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at zach@xekie.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of XEKIE after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, reach out to us at zach@xekie.com. We're a small team and we take privacy seriously.
