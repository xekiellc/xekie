Prohibited Items

Effective date: March 1, 2025  ·  XEKIE LLC

⚠️ The following items and categories are strictly prohibited on XEKIE. Posting for prohibited items will result in immediate removal and may result in account suspension or termination. When in doubt, don't post it.

⚠️
Weapons & Dangerous Items
Illegal firearms, unregistered weapons, or weapons modifications
Explosives, ammunition for illegal purposes, or materials to manufacture weapons
Knives or bladed weapons where prohibited by law
Tasers, stun guns, or other weapons where prohibited by local law
💊
Drugs & Regulated Substances
Illegal drugs or controlled substances of any kind
Prescription medications without a valid prescription
Drug paraphernalia intended for illegal drug use
Alcohol sales (regulated — requires proper licensing)
Tobacco products where prohibited by local law
🚫
Illegal & Stolen Goods
Stolen property of any kind
Counterfeit goods, fake brand items, or fraudulent products
Items that circumvent copyright or intellectual property protections
Pirated software, media, or digital content
Government-issued documents (IDs, passports, licenses)
🔞
Adult & Inappropriate Content
Adult content or services of a sexual nature
Any content involving minors in a sexual context (strictly prohibited and will be reported to authorities)
Escort services or solicitation
🐾
Animals
Live animals (connect with licensed breeders or shelters directly)
Endangered species or protected wildlife products
Animal parts where prohibited by law (ivory, certain furs, etc.)
💰
Financial & Personal Data
Credit card numbers, bank account information, or financial credentials
Personal data, databases, or hacked account information
Fraudulent currency or money laundering services
Pyramid schemes, multi-level marketing, or get-rich-quick schemes
☢️
Hazardous Materials
Hazardous chemicals, toxic substances, or radioactive materials
Items recalled for safety hazards
Medical waste or biohazardous materials

Enforcement

XEKIE reserves the right to remove any listing at any time for any reason, including listings that violate this policy. Violations may result in a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination depending on the severity.

We also cooperate with law enforcement when required and will report illegal activity when we become aware of it.

See something that shouldn't be here?

If you spot a listing that violates our policies, email us at zach@xekie.com with the details. We take reports seriously and will act quickly.
