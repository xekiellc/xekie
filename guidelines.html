Community Guidelines

Effective date: March 1, 2025  ·  XEKIE LLC

Our Community

XEKIE is built on trust between buyers and sellers. These guidelines exist to make sure XEKIE is a safe, fair, and useful place for everyone. We're a small team building something new — we appreciate every user who treats others with respect and uses the platform with good intentions.

For Buyers

✅ Do
  • Be specific about what you want — good details get better responses
  • Set a realistic budget for what you're asking for
  • Respond to sellers who take the time to reach out
  • Be honest about your location and shipping preferences
  • Update or delete your XEKIE when you've found what you need
✕ Don't
  • Post XEKIEs with unrealistic or bad-faith budgets
  • Post for prohibited items
  • Ghost sellers who respond to your posts
  • Post the same XEKIE repeatedly to spam the feed
  • Use XEKIE for anything other than genuine purchasing intent

For Sellers

✅ Do
  • Only respond if you genuinely have what the buyer is looking for
  • Be honest about the condition, history, and details of the item
  • Price fairly — buyers can see multiple responses
  • Be responsive once a buyer shows interest
  • Meet in safe, public locations for local transactions
✕ Don't
  • Misrepresent items or use misleading descriptions
  • Spam buyers with unsolicited offers outside their stated needs
  • Attempt to take transactions off-platform in a way that puts buyers at risk
  • Use XEKIE to solicit buyers for unrelated products or services
  • Respond to XEKIEs with intent to scam

Safety Tips

XEKIE connects strangers. Here are some common-sense safety tips:

  • Local meetups: Always meet in a public place — a coffee shop, police station parking lot, or busy store. Never meet at your home or theirs.
  • Payment: Use secure payment methods. Be wary of anyone asking for wire transfers, gift cards, or cryptocurrency. If it feels off, it probably is.
  • Inspect before paying: Always inspect the item before completing payment for local transactions.
  • Trust your gut: If something feels wrong about a transaction, walk away. No deal is worth your safety.
  • Shipping: Use tracked shipping and insured carriers for high-value items. Get proof of shipment.

Reporting Violations

If you see a XEKIE post, a seller response, or any user behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to us at zach@xekie.com. Include as much detail as possible — what you saw, when, and any relevant context.

We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly. Violations can result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans depending on severity.

Questions or concerns?

We're a small team that genuinely cares about this community. Reach us anytime at zach@xekie.com.
