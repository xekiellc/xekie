How it works

Inverted marketplace

1) Buyer posts what they want + price range
2) Sellers submit competing offers (best value wins)
3) Buyer selects an offer, then messaging unlocks
4) Requests expire to keep the marketplace fresh
Simple growth mechanic: Demand broadcasting — every request is shareable, so buyers recruit sellers.
